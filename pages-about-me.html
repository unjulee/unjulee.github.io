About Me

Unju Lee - Artist

a South Korean Sculptor based in NY.

I received my M.F.A. at Pratt Institute and officially began my métier in sculpture in 2001. Since then I've done a variety of exhibitions ranging from O.K. Harris, Broadway Windows, Washington Square Windows, New York Korean Cultural Center, and Mount Beacon Fine Arts.

My pieces all revolve around one material, each collection extending the long line of one central theme. This material is cardboard. I fell in love with the natural quality that formed from such a product of the synthetic era we live in.

Cardboard.

Defined as "pasteboard or stiff paper", the object of my art.
