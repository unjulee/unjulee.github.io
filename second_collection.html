The Second Collection


These are rough abstract ideas hewn into real pieces. They are considered a modern extension to the 365 Days Collection from 2008. They have considerably more variety in materials and shapes, breaking from the original constraint on primitive shapes.

These are also the most recent addition to the Unju Lee collection.

6" X 6" with varying depth
Made with various mediums including silicon bronze, aluminum, and copper.
